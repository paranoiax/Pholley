# PHOLLEY - A PHYSICS BASED VOLLEYBALL GAME

## What is Pholley?

Pholley is a physics based volleyballgame written in lua using the löve game engine.

It is currently under development.

However, the Title is still WIP (Work in Progress).

## How to play?

Simple: The goal of the game is to reach at least 5 points (with a difference of at least 2 from your enemy) before your enemy does.

You do so by kicking the ball on the ground of the oppent's half.

## Controls

* W - Jump
* A - Move to the left
* D - Move to the right
* R - Restart (After a round ended)
